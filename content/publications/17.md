abstract: "To comprehend the interactions between cancer cells, tumor-suppressor cells, immune-suppressor cells, and oncolytic viruses, the dynamical functions of neuroblastoma are described using a mathematical neuroblastoma model (NBLM). The study applies a Bayesian-Regularization Algorithm (BRA) based Stochastic Neural Networks (SNNs), implemented as BRA-SNNs, trained on datasets generated via the Runge Kutta Method (RKM) and divided into training, validation, and testing sets. Model performance is evaluated using Absolute Errors (AEs) and Mean-Square Errors (MSEs) to assess accuracy, efficiency, and predictive capability."
